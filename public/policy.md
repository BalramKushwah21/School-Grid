Privacy Policy (गोपनीयता नीति)
Last Updated: June 2026


At SCHOOL-GRID, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of schools, administrators, teachers, parents, and students.

1. Information We Collect
We collect data at different levels to run the software smoothly:

From School Admins: School Name, Email, Phone Number, Address, Logo, UDISE Code, and Payment Details.

From Teachers/Staff: Name, Contact Info, Qualification, and Attendance.

From Students/Parents: Name, Roll Number, Class, Guardian Details, Fees records, and Academic Performance.

Technical Data: IP Address, browser type, and cookies to keep users logged into their subdomains securely.

2. How We Use the Information
We use the collected information solely to:

Provide, maintain, and personalize the School Management Software.

Generate automated reports (Report cards, Fee receipts, Attendance charts).

Send critical SMS/Email alerts to parents (e.g., OTPs, fee reminders, emergency holidays).

Process subscription payments securely.

3. Data Protection and Security
Encryption: All data transferred between users and our servers is encrypted using SSL/HTTPS technology. Passwords are encrypted using secure hashing algorithms (like bcrypt) before saving to the database.

Access Control: Only authorized school users can view their specific school data. Our team cannot view student or financial data unless explicitly requested by the school admin for technical support.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We NEVER sell, trade, or rent school or student data to third-party marketing companies.

Data is only shared with trusted third-party services necessary for functionality, such as:

Payment Gateways (to process fee payments).

SMS/Email Gateways (to send school notifications).

5. Data Retention and Deletion
We retain school data as long as the subscription is active.

Upon termination or cancellation of the service, schools can request a complete export of their data. Following this, all data related to that school will be permanently deleted from our active databases within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy
Our platform processes data of minor students. However, this data is entirely entered and managed by the school authorities who possess the necessary legal rights and parental consent to do so. We do not directly interact with or collect data from children under 13 without school authorization.
